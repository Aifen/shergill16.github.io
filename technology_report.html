Snapchat

Product Specifications

Snapchat is an image messaging and multimedia mobile application that was created by Evan Spiegel,Bobby Murphy,and Reggie Brown.It was released on September,2011 seven years ago. Snapchat is a primarily used for creating multimedia messages that are referred to as snaps.The snaps can be edited by including filters,effects,text captions,and drawings. The ability of sending video snaps were added as a feature option in December,2012. Friends can be added by usernames and phone contacts,using “Snapcodes” or through “Add nearby” function. 30% of teenagers rank snapchat as their most important social network.

Target Market

30% of teenagers rank snapchat as their most important social network.Snapchat has a primary target market of over 50m 13 - 24 years olds that is presenting their largest users demographically. 55 % of Snapchat users are in the Us,with Europe making up the second largest geographic totaling 33% of all users. More 30+ year olds are beginning to use the service, but that usage is significantly lower. Catering to the 13-24 yo market is largely through speed of the transaction (typically a photo or video of short duration), and through the ephemeral nature of the communication.
