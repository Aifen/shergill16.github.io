How to shoot a Basketball

3 steps how to be a perfect shooter

Step:1- Having the Right Stance

Keep your feet no further than shoulder width apart, preferably closer together, and slightly staggered. Flex your knees slightly.Turn more in the air and jump forward, keeping your shoulders back, if you need to shoot a longer shot.

Step:2-Holding the Ball Correctly

Dip the ball down to your waist or thigh.Position your elbow so it's under the ball, not cocked to the side.Grip the ball correctly.

Step-3-Taking the shot

Jump slightly forward when you shoot, making sure your shoulders lean back and are relaxed.Push the ball upward with your shooting hand.Release the ball.Follow through.
